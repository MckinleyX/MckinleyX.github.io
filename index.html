General

glossary by me

A glossary of common math symbols

Competition Math for Middle School by J. Batterson

Teaches competition math for middle schoolers. Covers high-school level algebra and geometry, but most importantly it has an accessible introduction to counting (i.e. combinations, permutations, etc). Link is to a copy that you can borrow from archive.org.

Proofs

methods of proof by hdigh

An excellent, beginner-friendly introduction to four methods of proof (direct, contradiction, contrapositive, and induction) together with examples and great practice problems. This is blocked by FISD, if you're having trouble I have a mirror of it here.

induction by me

My handout on mathematical induction

Calculus

The Essence of Calculus by 3Blue1Brown

A video series on calculus with the goal that you come away feeling like you've invented calculus yourself, with lots of visuals and intuition about why things are the way they are.
